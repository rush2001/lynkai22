ERP SERVICES

Enterprise Resource Planning for growing businesses

What is an ERP System?

An ERP integrates all core business processes — finance, HR, inventory, procurement, sales, and more — into one unified platform, giving you real-time visibility across your entire operation.

What We Offer

  • Custom ERP development tailored to your industry
  • Implementation of leading platforms (Odoo, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics)
  • Module integration: Finance, HR, Inventory, CRM, Manufacturing
  • Data migration from legacy systems
  • Staff training and full onboarding
  • Ongoing maintenance and support contracts

AI Enhancement

Add AI-powered analytics, demand forecasting, and automated workflows to turn raw business data into actionable intelligence.

Pricing

Engagements start from €2,500. Contact us for a tailored quote.
